Flooring

Flooring is a crucial part of any interior design and can easily make or break your space. While our first interaction with a space is visual, the first physical contact a person will have with your space is the flooring. It serves as a foundation to your design and can ultimately impact its overall success.

Regardless of which design style you have chosen, selecting a complimentary flooring option is a must. As one of the most used surfaces in our home.

Nowadays, flooring is viewed as an investment and there are a number of options available for every budget. From hardwood, to laminate and vinyl we offer you a whole lot of options.


Vinyl Flooring


This can be supplied in rolls or tiles and is generally fixed by gluing. It can be manufactured in a very wide range of colours and profiles and as with laminate flooring can simulate the appearance of other materials.

Tiles Flooring
